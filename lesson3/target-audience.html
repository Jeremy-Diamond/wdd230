Target Audience

Our target audience will be split between our local business owners and their current/potential customers. There will be several small to medium businesses that will want to sponsor and help run several of our events througouth the year in order to drive name and brand recognition for them. Next to them we have several local sales representatives for various companies in our city that use our events for networking and driving business.

Persona Two- Local Sales Executive
