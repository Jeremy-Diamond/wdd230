Temple Weddings

With its great history and beautiful scenery, the Salt Lake City Temple is a great place to get married! Being located in the center of the city there are dozens of restaurants within walking distance. Also, being right next to the church office buildings there is a high likelihood that you will see one of the apostles and maybe even the prophet on your big day.
